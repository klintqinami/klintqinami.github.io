About

I work on machine learning compilers at Qualcomm R&D. Previously, I was at Reservoir Labs, Inc., working on compiler toolchains for machine learning, polyhedral compilers, and software verification. I have a strong interest in ML research, specifically in computer vision and natural language processing. I was briefly a Ph.D. student, working on bias mitigation in machine learning. Before that, I worked on computer graphics and geometry processing. I feel motivated when I can work on problems that have an impact in the world, and I like the engineering challenges that come with trying to make things work in practice. I like puzzles, and enjoy working hard on things that I find interesting.
